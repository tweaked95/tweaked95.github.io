About Me

Welcome to my page! I'm Nachiket Dabhade, a passionate Game Designer with an eagerness for creating immersive gaming experiences.

Ever since I got my hands on my first computer, I've been captivated by the world of games. I started by building card games as a young child and gradually progressed to working on intricate mechanics within game engines. Through this journey, I've come to truly understand and admire the dedication and creativity involved in developing exceptional video games. I am passionate about creating immersive experiences and games that transport people to new worlds and spark their imaginations! I've always been fascinated by the intersection of technology and creativity, and I love nothing more than using my skills to bring new ideas to life.

To equip myself with the necessary skills, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science to gain a solid foundation in game development. Subsequently, I enrolled in a Game Design program at LaSalle College, where I delved into the art of crafting engaging and compelling games. Finally, I completed a Design program at Algonquin College, which provided me with a well-rounded understanding of both the technical and artistic aspects of game development.

As a result, I consider myself a versatile Generalist. My proficiency in programming, design, and art allows me to contribute effectively to game development projects while appreciating the efforts of my fellow team members.

Beyond games, I have a keen interest in emerging technologies such as virtual cameras and virtual production in Unreal, which are revolutionizing the accessibility of film making for indie artists.

In addition to my gaming pursuits, I'm an amateur guitar player and find solace in spending time outdoors amidst the beauty of the mountains.

Thank you for visiting my page, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work and accomplishments in the world of game design!
